Backed by HeartMath® Science

HeartMath® is a research-based system of techniques, tools and technology. It helps wrestlers reduce stress, increase resilience, and improve focus. Using the emWave® Pro Plus, athletes can see their heart rhythms in real time and practice shifting into a coherent state.

  • Quickly re-balance mind, body and emotions as you reduce stress.
  • Build resilience through improved health, stamina and well-being.
  • Maximize creativity, focus and performance under pressure.
  • Be less reactive, think more clearly, and make better decisions in the heat of competition.

How It Works

We teach wrestlers step by step how to identify when they are incoherent, how to use simple HeartMath® tools to shift into coherence, and how to apply those tools during practice and matches.

  • Awareness: Recognize stress triggers and patterns.
  • Regulation: Use breathing and focus techniques to shift states.
  • Rehearsal: Practice in short, repeatable reps tied to real wrestling situations.

What Wrestlers Learn

  • Reset quickly between whistles and periods.
  • Maintain steady focus throughout long tournaments.
  • Recover emotionally and mentally after tough matches.
  • Develop inner confidence that shows up under pressure.

Programs

Group Training (Virtual)

Training together builds accountability and energy. Wrestlers learn HeartMath® tools in a group format and practice applying them in relation to wrestling.

  • Respond instead of react.
  • Sharper focus during practice and competition.
  • Greater resilience and faster recovery.

Private Training (Virtual/In-Person)

One-on-one sessions provide customized support for each athlete. Wrestlers master the techniques at their own pace and apply them to their unique goals.

  • Personalized coaching and feedback.
  • Deeper focus and clarity in high-pressure situations.
  • Tools to handle setbacks, maintain confidence, and alignment.
  • Ongoing accountability and mentorship support.

Team Workshops (In-Person)

Bring the training to your whole team. Coaches and wrestlers learn a shared language and practice coherence tools together.

  • Interactive sessions with live demonstrations.
  • Team-wide strategies for composure and focus.
  • Follow-up guidance to keep skills sharp all season.
