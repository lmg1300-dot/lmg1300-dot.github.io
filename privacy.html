Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

Cheagle LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) operates Wrestling With Coherence and values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information you voluntarily provide such as your name, email, and phone number.
  • Payment information processed securely through Stripe. We do not store full card details.
  • Session data (date, type of service, attendance) used to manage memberships and scheduling.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and manage training sessions or memberships.
  • To process payments and send relevant updates or confirmations.
  • To communicate with you regarding your program, sessions, or inquiries.

3. Data Protection

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal data. Payments are handled by Stripe, which meets PCI-DSS compliance standards.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information is only shared with trusted service providers such as Stripe or Google Workspace when necessary to deliver our services.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by emailing coachluis@wrestlingwithcoherence.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our practices or applicable laws change. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
