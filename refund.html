Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date:

This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines how refunds, cancellations, and scheduling changes are handled for programs offered by Cheagle LLC under Wrestling With Coherence.

1. Membership Commitments

  • Memberships are a monthly commitment billed bi-weekly through Stripe.
  • Memberships automatically renew unless canceled by providing written notice at least 48 hours before the next billing date.
  • Refunds are not issued for partial months or unused sessions once a billing cycle has started.

2. Cancellations

  • To cancel your membership, email lmg1300@gmail.com with at least 48 hours’ notice.
  • Failure to provide sufficient notice may result in being charged for the upcoming cycle.
  • You may request a membership pause with 48 hours’ notice — paused memberships will not be billed during that time.

3. Rescheduling

  • Private sessions may be rescheduled if at least 48 hours’ notice is provided by email.
  • Group sessions follow a fixed schedule and cannot be individually rescheduled or refunded if missed.
  • Late cancellations or missed private sessions without proper notice are considered forfeited.

4. Refund Exceptions

  • Refunds may be considered only in cases of duplicate billing or verified technical errors.
  • Requests must be made in writing within 7 days of the transaction date.

5. Contact

All refund, pause, or cancellation requests must be emailed to lmg1300@gmail.com.
